FAQ

Can it transfer to a human?

Yes. We can route urgent calls (or specific intents) to your team, while the assistant handles everything else.

Does it work after hours?

Yes. It can answer calls 24/7, capture leads, book appointments, and send you a summary the next morning.

Can it handle accents or multiple languages?

Yes. We can configure it for clear understanding and add multilingual support depending on your needs.

Do you store recordings?

Optional. We can store recordings and transcripts for quality and training, or disable storage for privacy-focused use cases.

How fast can we launch?

Typical pilots go live quickly once we collect your business details (hours, services, booking rules, and routing preferences).
