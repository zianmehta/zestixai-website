FAQ

Common questions

How long does implementation take?

Most clients have their first system live within 7 days. We handle all setup, prompt engineering, integrations, and testing — you don't need a tech team.

Will it work with our current tools?

In most cases, yes. We connect to CRMs, calendars, inboxes, and scheduling tools your team already uses — no rip-and-replace required.

What does it cost?

Every business is different. We build custom plans based on your specific workflows, team size, and goals. Book a free 30-min audit and we'll tell you exactly what makes sense for your situation.

Are you just a software vendor?

No — we're an implementation partner. We design the AI system, configure it, integrate it with your stack, and keep iterating based on real results. You get a partner, not a SaaS login.

What if the AI says something wrong?

We design every system with guardrails, escalation paths, and human handoff triggers. The AI knows its limits — and routes to a live person when it should.

Do I have to start with voice AI?

No. We start where the ROI is most obvious for your business. Sometimes that's voice, sometimes it's follow-up automation or internal tooling. We'll figure that out together on the audit call.
