FAQ

Are you only a call assistant company?

No. Voice AI is a flagship offer because the ROI is easy to understand, but ZestixAI also builds lead capture, workflow automation, and internal AI systems.

What should we start with first?

Usually the workflow with the clearest financial drag: missed calls, slow lead response, repetitive admin work, or messy customer handoffs.

In most cases, yes. The goal is to connect AI to your existing stack instead of forcing a full rip-and-replace.

Both. ZestixAI can help scope the right use case, design the workflow, build it, and improve it once real usage data starts coming in.
