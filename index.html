It means the system runs without human intervention. A customer calls, the system answers, qualifies them, books them, takes payment, and alerts your team â€” all while you sleep. No one is sitting at a desk managing it.

Absolutely. We recommend starting with the single biggest leak (usually calls). Once that's working, you expand to follow-up, internal tooling, etc.
